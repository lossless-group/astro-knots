Pattern source for any future slides-only Astro site, plus functionality to port into other sites that want to support deck iteration. Agent-skill created to codify and expedite proper setup. Spec at
`context-v/specs/Develop-a-Slides-only-Astro-Site-for-a-Fundraise-Process.md` — and a dedicated
`deck-iteration-workflow` skill encodes the variants/iteration motion.

This repos importance is in the "how" rather than the output.  Iteration cycles on getting Venture Capital decks playable from their website (or subdomain) yielded experience in what can both unleash generative creativity, as well as acheive breakneck speed in getting to a final product (with valuable option menus).

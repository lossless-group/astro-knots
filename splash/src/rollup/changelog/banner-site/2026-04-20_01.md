# Changelog - 2026-04-20

## Flag Pixel Spacing Variants

### Overview

Two new variants of FlagPixel that progressively reduce tile size and increase spacing, moving from a dense mosaic toward a sparse dot-matrix aesthetic. Both add rounded corners via ShapeGeometry.

---

## New Flare: FlagPixelLoose

Airy variant with smaller rounded-corner tiles and generous spacing.

- Tiles fill 48% horizontal / 36% vertical (vs 82% in FlagPixel)
- Vertical gaps roughly double horizontal gaps
- Rounded corners via `THREE.ShapeGeometry` with quadratic bezier curves
- Configurable `cornerRadius` prop (0-0.5, default 0.35)
- Same wave physics and parallax as FlagPixel

## New Flare: FlagPixelLooser

Sparsest variant — tiles shrink further with spacing increased by another third.

- Tiles fill 32% horizontal / 24% vertical
- Approaching a dot-matrix / sparse LED display look
- Default `cornerRadius` bumped to 0.4 (rounder at small size)
- Same wave physics and parallax as FlagPixel

## Tile Fill Progression

| Component | H Fill | V Fill | Character |
|---|---|---|---|
| FlagPixel | 82% | 82% | Dense mosaic |
| FlagPixelLoose | 48% | 36% | Airy tiles |
| FlagPixelLooser | 32% | 24% | Sparse dots |
